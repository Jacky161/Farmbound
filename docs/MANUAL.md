# Farmbound

You've been kidnapped! It's nothing personal really. We saw you as the right farmer for the job. Motivated, hard working. That's exactly what we need on our planet. We've got fertile land, you've got the skills. Do your job. Or else.

## Play Information

To keep things interesting, Farmbound features objectives provided by your managers. You can view your current task at any point by opening the menu, and scrolling to the second page. Beware! If you don't complete your task on time, it's game over!

## Getting Started

You'll first start at the farm area, but there's not much to do yet! The two main mechanics are farming and fishing. You'll need to head to town to buy seeds to plant and grow. The shopkeeper also sells a fishing rod, as well as some other useful goodies. Be sure to have a close look at his wares! The town has many characters that you can talk to that may provide helpful advice.

## Sleeping and Shipping

You can sleep by interacting with the house on the farm. Items can be shipped by interacting with the shipping box near the farm exit.

## Time

The day starts at 06:00 (6am). Your character will pass out at 00:00 (midnight). Make sure to be in bed by then!

## Stamina

There is a stamina bar in the lower right corner. This bar drains as you use items. If the bar fully depletes, you'll pass out!

## Passing Out

If you pass out, the game will progress to the next day. You'll lose 10% of your money, as well as only regaining 50% of your stamina to use the next day.

## Controls

A to interact with things and use items.

B to open the menu. You can scroll through the different menu pages by moving the cursor to the right and left.

D-pad moves the character around.

The crank is used situationally, depending on your items.

## Saving

Autosaving is on by default which will save your progress upon closing the game. You can choose to disable it via the menu button on the Playdate once in game. Manual saves can be created through this menu as well, though they will be overwritten by autosaves if enabled. If you have a save file to load, the game will prompt you upon opening the game if you would like to load it.
